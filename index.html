My Favorite Video Game

The Sims Game Series

The Sims is a series of games made by Electronic Arts (EA) that simulates life. You can create people and control their lives in your own mini world! Expansion packs add more fun with supernatural creatures, family expansions and hobbies, pets, as well as new careers.

The Sims games for purchase.

I have been playing the Sims games since I was little. I never played the very first game, but I played the second, third, and fourth. If I had to choose between all of them, I may pick the Sims 3.

Favorite Aspects

I love the games and all the things you can do, especially with addtional expansion packs. It would be almost impossible to rank them.

I particularly enjoy:

  • Character customization
  • Unique personalities
  • Creating lives different from my own
  • Expansion Packs
  • Hobbies

Final Thoughts

I grew up with these games. In recent years, I have played the Sims 4 msot often. However, I was able to find the Sims 2 and the Sims 3 on sale on the EA website. I have all the games except the first, since I am not very interested in its limitations.

For all the nostalgia and comfort possible, I will always come back the Sims games!
